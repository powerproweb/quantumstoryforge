AetherForge Worldbuilding Hub

This hub serves as the primary staging area for your steampunk science fiction universe. It’s where timelines, factions, technologies, and story engines are organized into a single, navigable control surface you can keep open while you write, sketch, or plan.

Universe Operations Layer

Instead of burying key information across scattered notebooks and files, the AetherForge Hub centralizes the spine of your world: major eras, anchor locations, signature technologies, and recurring character threads. You can link out to deep-dive documents, calculators, and tactical tools while this page remains the high-level dashboard.

Tools, Docs, and External Systems

Use this page to link directly into supporting modules such as the AetherForge Council of 8, specialized calculators, starship or weapon manuals, and any other narrative infrastructure you are building. Each linked tool can live on its own dedicated page while the Hub ties them into a coherent operational map.

Designed for Long-Arc Projects

The styling, color palette, and layout are tuned for long-form reading sessions and frequent reference checks. Dark backgrounds, brass-and-amber accents, and subtle gradients echo the steampunk tone without sacrificing clarity or readability on modern displays.
