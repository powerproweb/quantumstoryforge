What Is an ARC Reader?

An ARC Reader (Advance Review Copy Reader) is a trusted early reader who gets exclusive pre-release access to upcoming books. In exchange, ARC Readers offer feedback, post honest reviews, and help spread the word.

What You’ll Get

  • Free digital copies of every book, before launch day
  • Access to paperback and hardcover editions at cost
  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes peeks and maps
  • Printed thank-yous in select editions
  • First-access to all translations and spin-offs
